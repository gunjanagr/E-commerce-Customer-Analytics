# E-commerce Customer Analytics

Cluster 3 represents 15% of customers but drives 40% of revenue. This analysis identifies who they are, what they buy, and how to retain them — plus flags Cluster 5 as the highest churn risk.

## Business Questions Answered

- Which customers generate the most revenue, and what do they have in common?
- Which customer segments are at risk of churning?
- When should we increase inventory, and for which categories?
- Which regions should we prioritize for expansion?

## Key Findings

- **Cluster 3** (15% of base) drives 40% of total revenue — high-value, electronics-focused, Southeast Asia-heavy
- **Cluster 5** is the highest churn risk — longest time since last purchase, lowest average order value
- **Q4 drives 30% more transactions** than any other quarter — electronics inventory should increase 25% by October
- **65% of high-value customers** are from Southeast Asia — strongest market for fulfillment expansion

## Business Recommendations

1. Allocate retention budget to Cluster 3 first — losing 1 customer here = losing ~2.7x average customer value
2. Launch a re-engagement campaign for Cluster 5 before Q4 — discount + free shipping threshold
3. Stock electronics 25% higher from October — Q4 spike is consistent and predictable
4. Open next fulfillment center in Southeast Asia — 65% of premium customers are already there
